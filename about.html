About Opulent Sun Apparel

Opulent Sun Apparel is a family-owned and operated business that specializes in providing high-quality custom embroidery services. Based in Denver, Colorado, we serve local clients as well as customers from the continental US.

Our Mission

Our mission at Opulent Sun Apparel is to offer the best embroidery services in the industry while providing exceptional customer service at competitive prices. We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients by exceeding their expectations on every project.

Our Team

Our team consists of a very small group of highly experienced and skilled embroidery professionals who are passionate about their craft. They take pride in their work and are committed to delivering outstanding results for every project. We understand that each project is unique, and our main embroiderer will work closely with clients to ensure that their vision is brought to life.

Our Process

Our embroidery process starts with a consultation with our embroidery machine operator. During this phase, we take the time to understand the client's vision and preferences. We then create a custom design that meets their specifications and send it to them for approval. Once the design is approved, we use state-of-the-art equipment and the highest quality materials to bring the project to life. We carefully inspect every project to ensure that it meets our high standards for quality and workmanship.
